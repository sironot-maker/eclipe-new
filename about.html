OUR STORY

Family-Owned.
Ottawa Local.

Eclipse Kitchen & Bath Co. was founded with a simple vision: to bring exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service to every home renovation project in Ottawa. As a family-owned business, we understand that your home is more than just a space—it's where life happens.

7+ Years of Ottawa Expertise

With over seven years of specialized experience in kitchen and bathroom renovations, we've earned the trust of homeowners across Ottawa, Nepean, Kanata, Orléans, and beyond. Our deep understanding of local building codes, climate considerations, and architectural styles ensures every project is completed to the highest standards.

Our Approach

We believe that great design starts with listening. Every project begins with a detailed consultation where we learn about your vision, lifestyle, and budget. From there, our team of designers and craftsmen work together to create custom solutions that exceed expectations.

Design Excellence

Our in-house design team combines aesthetics with functionality, creating spaces that are both beautiful and practical for everyday living.

Quality Craftsmanship

Every cabinet, countertop, and fixture is installed with meticulous attention to detail by our experienced installation team.

Local Manufacturing

Our custom cabinetry is manufactured locally, ensuring quality control and supporting the Ottawa community.

Full-Service Renovation

From initial design to final installation, we manage every aspect of your renovation project.
